About

iProm, the night to be at, is a virtual gala sponsored
by the Apollo Foundation. This event is planned to reduce
the pressure several youngsters are facing, and to be able
to come together and celebrate without posing as a risk to
personal safety and health. The COVID-19 pandemic has led
to our lives coming to a halt, however with technology on our
side, we aim to create a stress-free environment and an
unforgettable night for all! We bring to you the iProm 2020
edition which will hopefully bring a smile to your face and
would allow you to take your mind off the worries and struggles
of today.furthermore, all the funds raised would be donated to
the Bagiya School, Gurgaon to help out the less fortunate,
especially during these unprecedented time
